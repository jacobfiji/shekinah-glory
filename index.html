Welcome to Our Family

A Church Built on Authentic Encounters With God

Shekinah Glory Fellowship Church is a vibrant, Spirit-filled community rooted in the Word of God. We believe every person who walks through our doors carries a God-given purpose waiting to be discovered, developed, and deployed.

Whether you're a lifelong believer or exploring faith for the first time, you will find a warm family here in the heart of Suva, Fiji — a family that worships freely, prays fervently, and loves unconditionally.
